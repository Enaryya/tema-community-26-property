Executive Summary

A Rare Institutional Asset
in a Growing Corridor

This property presents a compelling opportunity to acquire a well-advanced two-storey institutional building in Tema Community 26 — one of Greater Accra's fastest-growing urban zones.

Originally designed as a school campus, the building offers 20 distinct rooms across two floors, including classrooms, administrative offices, a large assembly hall, and washroom facilities with plumbing infrastructure already in place.

The structure is well advanced — with the reinforced concrete frame, roofing, plastering, windows, railings, and doors all completed. Interior finishing and landscaping remain outstanding, giving the incoming buyer full control over the final specification.

The land carries a 60-year leasehold tenure under TDC documentation with an approved site plan and clean title, providing long-term security for the buyer.
